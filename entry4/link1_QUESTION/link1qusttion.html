QUESTIONS TO HELP WITH PLANNING

Know yourself better and make your PLAN“JI”

    Q&A TO HELP WITH PLANNING

  1. Is this event/issue very important to you?

  2. Do you have enough time to deal with or decide on this problem?

  3. Are there other people and friends involved in this case?

  4. Is your interest or the interest of others more important in this case?

  5. Do you have any ideas about this case/issue?

  6. Have you made your plan (according to the logic of the flow chart)?

  7. In addition to this solution and solution you have thought of, do you have other alternatives?

  8. Is the current solution more appropriate or the alternative solution more appropriate?

  9. Are you ready to make your decision?

  10. Are you sure that this decision will not make you regret it?
